INSTRUCTIONS:

Open the F10 map, make sure "Show Drawings" filter is enabled on the top toolbar, and get familiar with all of the information presented there. Zoom out to see everything, and read each of the white boxes.
!NO EXCUSES!

After completing a race, you can view your race lines on the F10 map. They will remain there until the next race begins, or until you disconnect.

This mission will save your race data into a file so that your best time history is preserved. To make this work, you must desanitize your lua environment, done easily: Go to your DCS install folder, example: F:\DCS World\Scripts\MissionScripting.lua, and add a double dash in front of these two lines:
--sanitizeModule('io')
--sanitizeModule('lfs')
If running a dedicated server, you must do the same in its install folder.  
The save file will be created where your .miz is. Do not edit this file.

SRS FREQUENCIES:

Ch.1 (124 MHz) - Common traffic not in the race course.
Ch.2 (121 MHz) - Pilots active in the course or in formation with the pace plane.
Ch.3 (122 MHz) - Spare
Ch.4 (123 MHz) - Spare
---------------------------------------------------------------------
RACING RULES:

Passing:
An aircraft overtaken must not in any way impede or interfere with a faster overtaking plane. The overtaken pilot must keep the overtaken aircraft in sight at all times during the pass.

An aircraft over taking a slower airplane will not pass between the aircraft and a pylon. It must pass on the outside unless the overtaken craft is extremely wide and be kept in sight at all times during the pass by the overtaking pilot. 

If your engine quits or you receive a DNF (Did not finish), check your surroundings and turn out of the course immediately! Get out of the way of other racers.
---------------------------------------------------------------------
PYLON INFO:

Single pylons: Pass to the right of them.
Double pylons: Pass to the left of them.
Triple pylons: Always in pairs.  Pass between the two sets of 3 with wings level +/- 10 deg.

Horizontal requirement: Within 500 feet of single and double pylons
Vertical requirement: Below 500 feet AGL, marked by balloons.
---------------------------------------------------------------------
PENALTY AND BONUS INFO:

When the pace plane drops in, your position relative to the pace plane is evaluated:
  *Ahead of the pace? +2 sec plus 1 additional sec per every 65 feet
  *Above the pace? +2 sec + 1 additional sec per every 65 feet
  *Left of the pace? +2 sec
  *You will not be entered into the race if you are...
       ...more than 100 ft above or 500 feet below the pace, or
       ...more than 130 ft ahead of the pace, or
       ...left of the pace.
       ...(At the moment of drop-in)        

Pylon hit: +3 seconds (3 pylon hits and you are disqualified (DNF!))
(Note: Flying directly over a pylon is considered a hit!)
Missed pylon: +5 seconds (3 misses and you are disqualified (DNF!))
Too high at pylon: +2 seconds
Wings not level where required: +2 seconds

Flight through gate 8 below 50 feet: -1 second
Flight through gate 9 below 30 feet: -1 second
